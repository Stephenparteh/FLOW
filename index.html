About FLOW

FLOW (Forever Low-Cost Option for Water) was founded with a single mission: to provide efficient, sustainable access to safe drinking water for underserved communities. Through cutting-edge technology like solar-powered water extraction and IoT-based water monitoring, we are transforming how communities manage and consume water, reducing water emergencies by up to 51%. Our solutions not only ensure water safety but also optimize water distribution through innovative, user-friendly kiosks.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

At FLOW, we use a combination of solar-powered pumps, IoT sensors, and user-friendly kiosks to provide clean water to underserved communities. The system works in the following steps:
  • Water Extraction: We extract water from underground sources using solar-powered pumps, making the process energy-efficient and sustainable.
  • Water Quality Monitoring: IoT sensors monitor the water quality in real-time to ensure it meets safe drinking standards.
  • Water Distribution: Clean water is distributed via community kiosks, where residents can access it using mobile payments or prepaid cards.
  • Payment and Tracking: The system tracks water usage and allows users to pay through platforms like Orange Money, ensuring transparency and affordability.

FLOW is focused on providing safe drinking water to remote and underserved communities, particularly in Africa. Our primary areas of operation currently include rural regions in Liberia, where access to clean water has been a challenge. As we grow, we aim to expand our reach to other countries in need of sustainable water solutions.

Paying for water at a FLOW kiosk is simple and convenient. You have two main options:
  • Mobile Payment: You can pay for water using mobile money platforms such as Orange Money. Simply select the amount of water you need, make the payment via your mobile phone, and the kiosk will dispense the corresponding amount of water.
  • Prepaid Cards: For users without access to mobile payment services, we offer prepaid cards. These cards can be topped up with credit at designated locations, and then used at the kiosks to access water.
