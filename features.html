Our Features & Services

FLOW offers innovative, data-driven services to ensure sustainable water access to underserved communities.

Solar-Powered Kiosks

Our water kiosks are powered entirely by solar energy, making them a sustainable solution for areas with unreliable or no electricity. Solar panels ensure continuous operation, providing clean water even in remote locations.

Key Data: Powers up to 500 households daily, reduces carbon emissions by 30%.

IoT Sensors for Water Monitoring

IoT sensors in the kiosks monitor water quality in real-time, tracking indicators like pH, turbidity, and contaminants. This data helps ensure that only safe drinking water is provided and allows for prompt maintenance.

Key Data: Real-time updates every 5 minutes, reduces waterborne diseases by 40%.

Automated Water Distribution

Our automated system allows users to purchase water via mobile payment solutions like Orange Money. This reduces the need for human operators and ensures fair and accurate distribution based on payment.

Key Data: Dispenses up to 100 liters per transaction, reduces water wastage, integrates mobile payments.

Data-Driven Insights

Our platform provides detailed analytics on water consumption and system performance, enabling efficient resource management and helping us make data-driven decisions for scaling and improving our services.

Key Data: Real-time data and predictive analytics, reduces operational costs, optimizes system management.

Community Empowerment & Training

We provide training for local community members on kiosk management and maintenance, fostering ownership and ensuring long-term sustainability. Our program emphasizes skill development and local leadership.

Key Data: 70% of operators are local, 40% are female, training covers system maintenance and customer service.

Water Safety Standards & Certification

FLOW ensures that water quality meets international safety standards through regular testing and certification. Integrated filtration and UV purification processes are used to ensure safe drinking water.

Key Data: 99.9% of contaminants removed, monthly safety certifications, adheres to WHO standards.
